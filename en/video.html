Photos 4W Pro – Presentation Video

Photos 4W Pro is a Windows application designed to sort, organize and enhance your photos. This presentation video shows how to quickly classify your images by themes, locations or dates, display EXIF data, locate your shots on an interactive GPS map, generate instant thumbnails and browse your folders with remarkable fluidity. Whether you manage thousands of travel, family or work photos, Photos 4W Pro helps you find, sort, rename and explore your images with precision and simplicity. The application is available in French, English, German, Italian and Spanish. Designed for users who want to regain control of their photo collections and organize their memories!

The screenshots used in this tutorial are in English for practical reasons, but your application will appear in your own language.
